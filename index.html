Mortgages Performance and Machine Learning

2020 is proving to be an eventful year for both mortgage defaults and prepayments. Rates are at all time lows, defaults all time highs, and FED purchases are helping grease the tracks for a steady wave of refinancing. By taking advantage of data that was not available in 2003 or 2008, as well as using statistical techniques that were not available in those years we hope to get a better understanding of what is going on in the market. Below are samples of our work:
